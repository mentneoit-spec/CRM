## How to Access Send Marks Email

### Method 1: From Main Dashboard (NEW!)
1. Login at http://localhost:3002
2. You'll see the main dashboard with stats cards
3. Below the stats, there's a **"Quick Actions"** section
4. Click the purple **"Send Marks Email"** button
5. You'll be taken directly to the marks email page

### Method 2: From Sidebar Menu
1. Click on **"Academics"** in the left sidebar
2. Click on **"Send Marks Email"**

### Method 3: Direct URL
Go to: http://localhost:3002/admin/send-marks-email
